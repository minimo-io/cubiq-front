## Developer Resources

- [nostr.net](https://nostr.net/): A collection of projects and resources built on nostr to help developers and users.
- [NDK](https://github.com/nostr-dev-kit): Nostr Development Kit by pablof7z.

## Apps & Services

- [nostr.watch](https://nostr.watch/): Find fast nostr relays.
- [habla.news](https://habla.news/faq): A nostr-based web app that enables anyone to earn from their writing.
- [nostrapps.com](https://www.nostrapps.com/): A curated list of Nostr apps.
- [nostrsync.live](https://nostrsync.live/): Download a copy of your Data.
- [zaplife.lol](https://zaplife.lol/): Zapping stats.
- [stats.nostr.band](https://stats.nostr.band/): Nostr Real Time Stats.
- [nostrends.vercel.app](https://nostrends.vercel.app/): What's trending on Nostr?
- [nostr-followback](https://github.com/minimo-io/appticles/tree/main/nostr-followback): Who's not following you back?
